NYU

NYU is considered to be one of the top medical schools in the US. For this reason I believe it can be one of the best opportunities for me to go into plastic surgery. Out of state universities is something I have always looked into when speaking about universities. I love the idea of experiencing new things or trying something new and being independent is something I for sure see as a big step forward.

Tution

56,500 USD

Requirements To Apply

  • High school transcript
  • A Self Reported Academic Record (SRAR)
  • One or two teacher letters of recommendation
  • Test scores to satisfy NYU's flexible testing policy
